<h1>Kallvik Surf Ry</h1>

<p class="lead">
Finland's largest surf club, with around 200 active surfers. The club is based both in Kallvik (Helsinki) and Hanko.
</p>

<div class="secondary-info">
<p>
<strong>The club was founded in 1985</strong>, led by active surfers from eastern Helsinki.
</p>
<p>
As a Kallvik Surf member, you can try and train with the club's boards and equipment, and get guidance on using them safely and correctly.
</p>
<p>
<strong>Announcements and discussion</strong> happen in the club's
<a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/115506131814051">Facebook group</a>.
</p>
</div>
