# Become a member

<p class="lead">
Interested in joining? Kallvik Surf ry offers you and your family an easy way to enjoy this wonderfully refreshing sport in Helsinki at Kallahti.
</p>

<div class="card">

### Sign up online

The easiest way: give us your email address and we'll send you a link to fill in your member details. You'll receive the membership invoice straight to your inbox.

<a href="https://club.kallviksurf.fi/join" class="cta-button cta-primary">Join online</a>

You can also use the bank details below directly if you prefer to pay by bank transfer.

</div>

## What does membership offer?

<div class="card">

**As a Kallvik Surf member:**
- You can try and train with the club's equipment
- You get guidance on using the gear
- You can rent a container spot for your equipment right next to the beach
- You're part of Finland's largest surf club (~200 members)

</div>

## Membership fees for the season

<div class="card">

| Member type | Price |
|-------------|-------|
| **Membership** | 30 € |
| **Family membership** | 35 € |
| **Junior membership** | 10 € |
| **Container spot** (handled by container officer) | 70 € |
| **Supporting membership** | 10 € or more |

**Note:** There is no separate joining fee.

⚠️ **Payment schedule:** The seasonal membership fee and the container spot renewal must be paid to the club's account **by May 31st**.

</div>
