## Locations

### Helsinki - Kallahti peninsula

<div class="card">

The main site and surf hub is on the Kallahti peninsula in Helsinki.

**[See on map →](http://maps.google.fi/?ie=UTF8&ll=60.187996,25.138714&spn=0.002571,0.006695&t=h&z=17)**

<div style="display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fit, minmax(200px, 1fr)); gap: 1rem; margin-top: 1rem;">

![Container](/galleria/kontti.jpg)

![Gate](/galleria/portti.jpg)

</div>

**Surf areas:**

Kallahti bay, the waters around Kallvik point, and Iso Kallahti (Apinalahti) offer excellent conditions for windsurfing and kiteboarding. More experienced riders sail out between Itä Villinki and Kuiva-Hevonen, past Tammaluoto, all the way to Eestiluoto.

![Kallvik surf areas](/galleria/KallvikSurffialueet_map.gif)

**Wind conditions:**

Winds from the northwest to northeast don't work for this beach. The nearest beach suited to a northeasterly wind is the shallow sandy beach at the head of Riveliniemi, on the other side of the peninsula. Watch out for underwater rocks near the shore.

</div>

## Safety

<div class="card">

⚠️ **Important reminders:**

- Never head out to sea alone — always go with a group
- Equipment failure or other accidents can otherwise become fatal
- In the old days, riders did long-board tours around Harmaja and even coffee runs to Kaunissaari, but always with safety first!

</div>
